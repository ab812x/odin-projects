Chicken Alfredo

This creamy Chicken Alfredo is a rich and comforting pasta dish made with tender chicken breast, garlic, butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. Perfect for dinner and easy to prepare at home.

    Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  1. step 1
    Gather all your ingredients.
  2. step 2
    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. step 3
    In a large pan over medium heat, cook the chicken slices until golden brown and fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  4. step 4
    In the same pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for about 1 minute.
  5. step 5
    Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. step 6
    Stir in Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  7. step 7
    Return the cooked chicken to the pan and mix well.
  8. step 8
    Add the cooked pasta and toss everything together.
  9. step 9
    Season with salt and black pepper.
  10. step 10
    Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
