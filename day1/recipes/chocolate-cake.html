Chocolate Cake

This homemade chocolate cake is moist, fluffy, and full of rich chocolate flavor. It's perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or anytime you crave something sweet.

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  1. step 1
    Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. step 2
    Grease and flour a baking pan.
  3. step 3
    In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  4. step 4
    Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  5. step 5
    Stir in boiling water carefully until smooth.
  6. step 6
    Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  7. step 7
    Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. step 8
    Let it cool before serving.
